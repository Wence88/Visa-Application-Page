Consent

If your application is succesful, there will be conditions on your visa. This will include, for example, whether you are able to work in Ilawe Ekiti.

If you stay in Ilawe Ekiti without permission:

  • You can be detained
  • You can be prosecuted, fined and imprisoned
  • You can be removed and banned from retuning to Ilawe Ekiti
  • You will not be allowed to work
  • You will not be able to rent a home in Ilawe Ekiti
  • You can be denied access to a bank account
